# Deep Learning Course – Information Page

A repo for all Deep learning Course, exercises, and assignments.

## Overview

* **Duration:** 14 weeks
* **Structure:**

  * **Lecture:** 4 hours (Fridays, 8:00–12:00, Physics Auditorium 1523-318)
  * **Exercise:** 2 hours (different rooms/times, check [timetable.au.dk](https://timetable.au.dk))
* **Format:**

  * Weekly lecture introducing theory + exercises
  * Exercise sessions focus on Q\&A with TAs
* **Note:** No exercise session in Week 1.

## Curriculum

* **No textbook required** – detailed slides + online resources provided.
* Recommended reference: [Dive Into Deep Learning](https://d2l.ai/).
* Weekly readings split into **Required** and **Optional**.
* Hands-on learning emphasized through exercises and PyTorch documentation.

## Assessments

* **Assignments (x2):**

  * Ungraded, must be passed individually to continue.
  * Group discussions allowed, but submissions are individual.
* **Final Project:**

  * Group project (≤4 students) with self-chosen data & task.
  * Graded.
  * Details in “Final Project” section.

## Key Dates

* **Assignment 1 Deadline:** 2/10 (night before Week 6 lecture)
* **Assignment 2 Deadline:** 6/11 (day before Week 10 lecture)
* **Pitch Upload Deadline:** 12/11
* **Feedback Deadline:** 14/11
